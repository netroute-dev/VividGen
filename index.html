Heads Up! Key Points for a Smooth VividGen Experience

To ensure you get the most out of VividGen and understand its unique design, please take a moment to review these important considerations:

  • •
    No History, Maximize Creativity: Past generations are not stored to keep your creative canvas fresh and focused on new prompts. Remember to save your stunning images manually!
  • •
    Secure Access Keys: Each Access Key is for a single session. Once you log in, it's consumed. A brief 15-minute lockout after logging out helps prevent unauthorized sharing.
  • •
    Desktop-Optimized Performance: For the best quality and performance, VividGen is designed exclusively for desktop usage. Mobile devices are not supported.
  • •
    Fair Usage for All: While you enjoy extensive usage, our system monitors for unusually long generation times to ensure fair access and optimal performance for every user.
  • •
    No Image Swapping: Uploading existing images or attempting to swap elements is not permitted. This maintains consistency and adheres to our generation guidelines.
